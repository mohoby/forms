1. Q: How do I get in?

 A: Click on SIGN UP>> on top of http://MoSouls.com or >> Join NOW! under Member Login. Follow 2 steps to register, if you quit ater step 1, you won't be able to use your registered email address. 2. Q: When I login, it always show "error" page

 A: You may hit security settings for repeated login, refresh the browser or bring up a new browser, type in: http://MoSouls.com, you will be able to login. If you forgot your password, click on "forgot password" link under the login box, the password will email to you. If you forgot both user id and password, contact us with your nickname, birthday, security answer, cellphone number.

3. Q: How to view online members?

 A: from chat, you can find "Who's in the Chat Room" title on the left, it will show you all member's in the chat rooms.

4. Q: How do I add a member to my friends?

 A: from emails or connections, you can find "add as a friend" button, select the person who send you an email or gift by a member's profile picture, and click the button, the friend request will be sent to the member. You can also add a friend from any kind of search, click on friend icon, your friend request will be sent to the member. To remove a member from your list, go to home, click on friends link on the left, and click "delete" under the picture or nickname, the member will be deleted from your friend list.

5, Q: How to use email, connection?

7. Q: How to use instant messaging?

 A: Instant message is for online chat, simply click on enter button on the left of chat page. If a security box pop up, click on Ok button. Some computers already have HotSpot java installed(such as new Dell computers or new computer with IE8), You do not need to install Java plugin.
